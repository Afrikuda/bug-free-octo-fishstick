Practical support for growing organisations.

Afrikuda Consulting provides governance, systems, programme design, evaluation, resource mobilisation, and practical operational support for NGOs, civil society organisations, funders, and small businesses across Africa.

Afrikuda also offers AfriOps — a practical systems and operations support service designed to help organisations and businesses work more efficiently, strengthen internal processes, reduce pressure on one or two people, and make better use of what they already have.

About Us

Practical support shaped by organisational experience.

Afrikuda Consulting provides practical support to organisations and businesses that need stronger governance, clearer systems, and more efficient ways of working.

Our work is informed by more than 30 years of entrepreneurial experience, more than 20 years in IT, and 15 years of experience in senior management, programme design, evaluation, systems, governance, and operational support.

Afrikuda was established in response to a clear gap. Many organisations and businesses are under pressure to do more with limited staff, time, and resources, but the support available is often too generic, too expensive, or too removed from operational reality to be genuinely useful. Our approach is grounded in practical support that helps teams work more effectively with what they already have.

This includes helping teams review how work is currently being done, identify pressure points, and put in place practical systems, clearer processes, and simple automations that make work easier to manage and easier to sustain over time.

Who can benefit from this

Afrikuda works with NGOs, civil society organisations, and small businesses, particularly those that are growing, formalising systems, navigating change, or trying to work more effectively with limited time and resources.

This work is also relevant to funders that want to support grantees to do more with what they have by strengthening internal systems, reducing inefficiencies, and improving day-to-day operational effectiveness.

You may need this support if

  • Your systems depend too heavily on one or two people.
  • Key processes are inconsistent or not documented.
  • Governance and compliance obligations are difficult to manage.
  • Programme design or evaluation feels unclear or underdeveloped.
  • Fundraising is reactive rather than strategic.
  • Reporting takes too much time or creates unnecessary pressure.
  • Your organisation or business is growing, but internal systems are not keeping pace.
What we do

Two distinct service areas.

Afrikuda provides two distinct service areas: Consulting Services and AfriOps. AfriOps has been tailored separately for NGOs and civil society organisations, and for small businesses, because the pressures, reporting demands, and operational needs are not the same.

Consulting Services

Tailored support for organisations that need stronger foundations, clearer systems, and more effective ways of working.

  • Governance & Compliance — support with governance structures, accountability processes, compliance requirements, and the practical systems needed to manage them well.
  • Programme Design & Evaluation — support with programme design, programme thinking, evaluation approaches, and the systems needed to make delivery and learning more coherent.
  • Resource Mobilisation — support with fundraising thinking, resource mobilisation approaches, and the systems that help organisations engage more strategically with funding pressures.
  • Partnerships & Stakeholder Management — support with partnership processes, relationship management, and clearer ways of working across different stakeholders.
  • Organisational Systems — support with internal systems, practical workflows, process development, and ways of working that are clearer, more efficient, and easier to sustain over time.
AfriOps · NGOs & Civil Society

AfriOps is Afrikuda’s practical systems and operations support service. This stream has been tailored for NGOs and civil society organisations that need stronger workflows, clearer internal processes, and more manageable systems.

This stream is particularly useful where reporting processes are heavy or inconsistent, programme tracking is unclear or inefficient, governance administration is difficult to manage, internal processes are not well documented, too much operational knowledge sits with one or two people, or teams need low-cost, practical systems that can be used consistently.

  • Where Does the Time Actually Go? — identifying pressure points, inefficiencies, and patterns that make work harder to manage than it needs to be.
  • Practical Tools That Work for You — looking at accessible tools and systems that can support coordination, communication, tracking, and day-to-day workflow management.
  • Donor Comms and Reporting Without Drowning — strengthening reporting and donor communication processes so they are more manageable, consistent, and less draining on teams.
  • Programme Tracking Without Expensive Software — putting in place practical ways to track programme work clearly without relying on costly systems.
  • Board Admin and Governance Made Simple — strengthening governance administration so that board processes, accountability, and documentation are easier to manage.
  • Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) Your Team Will Actually Use — developing practical internal procedures that can be used consistently and do not just sit on paper.
AfriOps · Small Businesses

AfriOps is Afrikuda’s practical systems and operations support service. This stream has been tailored separately for small businesses because the pressures, operational needs, and ways of working are different.

This stream is designed for small businesses that need stronger routines, clearer workflows, and more efficient day-to-day systems.

  • Audit Your Week — identifying where time is being lost and where systems need tightening.
  • Practical Tools That Work for You — looking at accessible tools that can support scheduling, coordination, communication, invoicing, and workflow management.
  • Automating Client Follow-Up, Invoicing and Scheduling — using simple systems and automations to reduce repetitive admin and improve consistency.
  • Stock, Suppliers and Admin Without the Chaos — putting in place clearer ways to manage operational flow and routine administration.
  • Getting AI to Do the Grunt Work — using practical AI and automation support to reduce low-value repetitive tasks.
  • Building Systems So the Business Doesn’t Run Only on You — strengthening internal systems so the business is less dependent on one person holding everything together.
