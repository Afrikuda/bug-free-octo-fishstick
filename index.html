About Afrikuda

Built from the inside out.

Afrikuda is a governance and strategy consultancy with over 15 years of experience supporting organisations across civil society and the private sector in Africa.

We bring legal, programme, and operational expertise to the challenges organisations face every day. Our work is grounded in lived experience. We have worked inside the organisations we now support, leading programmes, managing budgets, navigating governance, and building systems under real pressure.

We work across governance and compliance, programme design and evaluation, resource mobilisation, partnerships, and operational systems. We focus on solutions that are practical, affordable, and built to last.

What we stand for

  • EmpowermentWe believe organisations should have the tools they need to do their work well.
  • IntegrityWe are honest about what is possible. We do not oversell and we do not disappear after delivery.
  • CollaborationWe work with organisations, not at them. The best solutions come from inside.
  • ImpactWe measure our success by what changes in practice, not only by what we deliver.
  • InnovationWe keep looking for better ways to work, especially when resources are tight.

AfriOps · Stream 01

NGOs and Civil Society

Do More With Less · Wednesdays 10:00–12:00 SAST

AfriOps is a live, cohort-based training offer. This stream is for NGOs and civil society organisations that want stronger systems without expensive software or duplicated effort.

Format

6 weekly live online sessions. 2 hours per session on Google Meet.

First cohort

Week of 8 June 2026. Registration closes 31 May 2026.

Certificate

Participants attending 5 of 6 sessions receive an AfriOps Certificate of Completion.

Pricing

From R500 per module or R2,000 for the full stream.

Modules

  • Where Does the Time Actually Go?
  • Your Free Tech Stack
  • Donor Comms and Reporting Without Drowning
  • Programme Tracking Without Expensive Software
  • Board Admin and Governance Made Simple
  • SOPs Your Team Will Actually Use

How it works

  • Minimum 10, maximum 20 participants per stream.
  • Each session ends with a short preparation task.
  • A one-page reference document is shared after every session.
  • Sessions are not recorded.

Investment

  • Single module — per seat: R500
  • Any 3 modules — per seat: R1,200
  • Full stream (6 modules) — per seat: R2,000
  • Closed cohort — full stream: R18,000 flat

Founding cohort

Founding organisations receive a 15% discount on full stream bookings in exchange for a written testimonial within two weeks of completing the programme.
