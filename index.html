About Afrikuda

Practical support shaped by organisational experience.

Afrikuda Consulting provides practical support to organisations that need stronger governance, systems, programme design, evaluation, and resource mobilisation. Afrikuda also offers AfriOps training for teams that need practical systems that are affordable, usable, and built for day-to-day realities.

Afrikuda is informed by experience working inside organisations, not only advising them from the outside. That experience includes programme leadership, governance processes, partnership management, systems development, and the operational demands that shape how organisations function in practice.

Afrikuda was established in response to a clear gap: many organisations need support that is practical, context-sensitive, and grounded in how work actually happens. Too often, services are too generic, too expensive, or too removed from operational reality to be genuinely useful.

Afrikuda works with NGOs, civil society organisations, and small businesses, particularly those that are growing, formalising systems, navigating change, or trying to work more effectively with limited time and resources.

You may need this support if

  • Your systems depend too heavily on one or two people.
  • Key processes are inconsistent or not documented.
  • Governance and compliance obligations are difficult to manage.
  • Programme design or evaluation feels unclear or underdeveloped.
  • Fundraising is reactive rather than strategic.
  • Reporting takes too much time or creates unnecessary pressure.
  • Your organisation is growing, but internal systems are not keeping pace.
